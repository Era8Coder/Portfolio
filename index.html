My Projects

Discrete Time Markov Chain(DTMC) using Hidden Markov Models

We need to analyze specific types of distributions, focusing solely on Gaussian distributions, to predict whether the market is "Bullish" or "Bearish." For this, we employ the Baum-Welch Algorithm, which is a special case of the Expectation-Maximization Algorithm. The hidden states of the model are represented in a transition matrix, and the observations follow a Gaussian distribution. Our goal is to identify the local maxima where the probability of the observations is at its peak. Used Python Libraries NumPy, Pandas and Random.
